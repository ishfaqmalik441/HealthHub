Welcome to HealthHub

Your one-stop solution for managing fitness, health, and overall well-being.

With a variety of interactive tools and visualizations, we empower you to achieve your fitness goals while maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Our app is tailored to help you track your progress, stay motivated, and make informed decisions about your health.

Features

Body Mass Index (BMI) Analysis: Gain valuable insights into your BMI trends, track weight changes, and categorize your health status (underweight, normal, overweight, or obese). This tool also provides personalized statistics to help you stay within a healthy range.

Diet Plan Tracker: Stay on top of your nutrition by uploading your diet plan and monitoring your caloric intake. Compare your actual diet with a personalized target, and visualize your progress with easy-to-understand charts.

Workout Radar: See the distribution of your workouts across different body groups with our Radar feature. This helps you identify strengths and areas to improve for a more balanced fitness routine.

Interactive Workout Calendar: Plan and track your workout routines effortlessly with our interactive calendar. Visualize your progress, maintain consistency, and break your fitness goals into achievable milestones.
